Privacy Policy

Boleiros App · Last updated April 27, 2026
🇧🇷 Português 🇺🇸 English
One-line summary: Boleiros does not collect any personal data from you. Everything you play is stored locally on your own device, and nothing is sent to external servers.

1. Who we are

The Boleiros app ("App", "we") is developed and maintained by Boleiros ("developer"). This policy describes how the App handles user information ("you").

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at fvsp.digital@gmail.com.

2. What data we collect

None. Boleiros is designed to run entirely on your device, with no communication to external servers. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, location, device identifiers, or any other personal information.
  • We do not require registration, login, or any form of authentication.
  • We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics SDKs, or advertising tools.
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, contacts, calendar, photos, or other sensitive device data.

3. Where your game data is stored

All your career progress (saves, achievements, season history, settings) is stored exclusively in your device's local storage (a technology called localStorage in web browsers).

This data:

  • Stays only on your device;
  • Is never sent to us or to any third party;
  • Is automatically removed if you uninstall the App or clear your browser/app data.

4. Community Packs (optional DLC)

The App allows you to import optional content packs (called "Community Packs" or "DLC") with custom team crests and player names. These packs:

  • Are JSON files that you download and import manually;
  • Are not provided by us and are not part of the App distributed in stores;
  • When imported, are stored only on your device, just like your saves.

You are responsible for the origin and legality of the packs you import. We are not responsible for content in packs developed by third parties.

5. Data sharing

Since we do not collect any data, there is no sharing with third parties, partners, advertisers, or any other entity.

6. Children and minors

Boleiros is rated suitable for all audiences, including children. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user (including those under 13), the App complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) in the United States and the GDPR-K (children-related provisions of the EU General Data Protection Regulation).

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has involuntarily shared personal data with us (for example, by contacting us by email), please reach out to the address above and we will delete any information immediately.

7. Security

Since no data of yours is transmitted to or stored by us, there is no risk of leakage or breach related to data handling by the developer. Game data stored on your device is protected by the security mechanisms of the operating system (Android, iOS) and the browser itself.

8. Your rights

Although we don't have your data, you have rights guaranteed by GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), LGPD (Brazil), and other applicable legislations, including:

  • Access: knowing what data we have about you (answer: none);
  • Correction and deletion: not applicable, as we do not store your data;
  • Portability: your game saves can be exported/imported manually through the App's own features;
  • Withdrawal of consent: not applicable, as there is no collection subject to consent.

To delete all game data on your device, simply uninstall the App or clear its data through your operating system's settings.

9. App permissions

Boleiros does not request any sensitive device permissions. In particular, we do not request access to:

  • Location (GPS or network);
  • Camera or microphone;
  • Contacts, calendar, messages;
  • External storage (except to manually import optional pack files you downloaded yourself);
  • Advertising or device identifiers.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally, for example to reflect changes in the App or in applicable legislation. Whenever there are relevant changes, we will update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. The most recent version will always be available at the public URL of this policy, indicated on the App's Google Play Store listing.

We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.

11. Contact

For any questions, requests, or complaints related to this Privacy Policy or to data handling by the App, please contact:

  • Developer: Boleiros
  • Email: fvsp.digital@gmail.com
